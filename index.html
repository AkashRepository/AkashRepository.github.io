Indian Patents Law
This module apprises Indian Patents Law and procedure. The modules strive to deliver in-depth knowledge of Patent filing procedures associated timelines prescribed in Indian Patents Act, 1970 and Indian Patents (Amendment) Rules 2016.

Indian Copyrights Law
This module apprises Indian Copyrights Law and procedure. The modules strive to deliver in-depth knowledge of Patent filing procedures associated timelines prescribed in Indian Copyright (Amendment) Act 2012 and Indian Copyright Rules (Amendment) 2016

Indian Trademarks and Unfair Competition Law
This module apprises Indian Trademarks Law and procedure. The modules strive to deliver in-depth knowledge of Trademarks filing procedures associated timelines prescribed in Indian Trade Marks (Amendment) Act, 2010 and Indian Trade Marks Rules, 2017. Also, this module describes various aspects of unfair competition law.

Indian Designs Law
This module apprises Indian Designs Law and procedure. The modules strive to deliver in-depth knowledge of Design filing procedures associated timelines prescribed in Indian Design Act, 2000 and Indian Design (Amendment) Rules, 2014

Indian Geographical Indications Law
This module apprises about Indian Geographical Indications Law and procedure. The modules strive to deliver in-depth knowledge of Geographical Indications filing procedures associated timelines prescribed in Indian The Geographical Indications of Goods (Registration & Protection) Act, 1999 and Indian The Geographical Indications of Goods (Registration & Protection) Rules, 2002.
