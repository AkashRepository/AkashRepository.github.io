About Us

Centre for Intellectual Property Consulting and Training (CIPCT) is focussed on providing well-planned courses by highly experienced consultants that can help boost your career in Intellectual Property (IP). The courses provide comprehensive knowledge about different types of IP like patents, trademarks, copyrights and design. The courses range from a variety of levels like freshers, beginners, experienced, and advanced. The program is designed for engineers, science, law graduates, post graduates, Phds etc.

Vision

Skill based industrial oriented training to students and professionals that cater to achieve industrial requirements and employment.
