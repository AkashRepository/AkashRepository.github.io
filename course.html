Course Methodology

The course is conducted in three comprehensive stages designed to provide a complete learning experience:

Stage 1: Study Material & Interaction

Soft copy of Study Material will be provided to Students along with case studies to enable participants to raise queries and interact with other participants. The queries raised by participants would be responded by IPR Experts.

Stage 2: Expert-Led Sessions

Webinars and online lectures will be delivered by senior industry experts and academicians. Includes case study analysis and practical assignments.

Stage 3: Validation & Certification

Validation points of case studies and issuance of Certificate upon successful completion of the course requirements.

Registration Procedure:

  • Those interested are required to visit our website www.cipct.co.in and follow the link, fill the online Registration Form available on the website.
  • Course fee, as applicable, is to be made through PayTM, UPI and online payment methods.
