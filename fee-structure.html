Course and Fee Structure

Our comprehensive IPR training programs are designed to provide expert guidance and practical knowledge in various aspects of Intellectual Property Rights. We offer specialized courses in Patent Searching, Patent Drafting, Patent Infringement Analysis, and Patent Prosecution, each tailored to meet the specific needs of professionals and students in the IP domain.

Our expert-led sessions combine theoretical knowledge with hands-on practice, ensuring participants gain practical skills that are immediately applicable in real-world scenarios. With flexible duration options ranging from 1 to 8 hours, our courses accommodate different learning schedules and experience levels.

Our team of seasoned IPR professionals and industry experts provide personalized guidance and career counseling to help you navigate the complex world of intellectual property. Whether you're a beginner looking to understand patent basics or an experienced professional seeking advanced training, our structured fee system ensures quality education at competitive prices.
